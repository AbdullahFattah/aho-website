How it works

Every hunt is a puzzle with a real-world answer. Solve it, get there before anyone else, and the prize is yours.

1

Study the clue

Each hunt drops with a photo, hints, or a riddle. Look closely — the location is hiding in plain sight.

2

Go find it

Travel to where you think the spot is. The prize amount drops each day, so racing other hunters matters.

3

Submit and win

Snap a photo at the exact spot. We verify your GPS, you win the prize. Your name and photo go on the spotlight.

Three types of hunts

Different puzzle styles for different moods. New hunts drop weekly.

🗺️

Geographic

A photo of a real place. Identify it, travel there, prove you made it.

🧩

Riddle

No travel required. Solve the riddle, submit your answer, our team verifies.

⚡

Flash riddle

Same as riddles, but the clock is in hours, not days. First to crack it wins.
