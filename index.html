How it works

New puzzles drop on a schedule - geographic hunts, daily and weekly riddles, and flash riddles that close in hours. Whichever you pick, the flow is the same.

1

Pick a puzzle

Open Aho and choose what's live: a photo of a real place, a daily or weekly riddle, or a flash riddle on a short timer.

2

Crack it

Geographic hunts: figure out where the photo was taken and go there. Riddles and flash riddles: solve them from anywhere - picture, words, lateral thinking.

3

Submit first, win

Geographic: snap a photo at the spot, GPS verifies it. Riddles: send your answer, our team reviews. The first valid submission takes the prize.

Three types of hunts

Different puzzle styles for different moods - something live every day.

🗺️

Geographic

A photo of a real place. Work out where it was taken, travel there, and submit a photo at the spot to prove it. GPS-verified.

🧩

Daily & weekly riddles

No camera, no travel. Just a riddle - sometimes with a picture or other clues - that you solve from your couch. Send the answer, our team checks it.

⚡

Flash riddles

Same idea as a normal riddle, but the window is tiny - minutes to hours. First to crack it walks away with it.
