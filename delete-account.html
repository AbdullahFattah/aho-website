Delete your Aho account

Last updated: 18 May 2026

You can delete your Aho account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it - the in-app option is fastest and recommended. The email option is a fallback for users who can't access the app anymore.

Option 1 - In the app (recommended)

This is the fastest and most secure path. Signing in already verifies that you own the phone number on the account, so we can process the deletion immediately without any extra checks.

  1. Open Aho and sign in.
  2. Go to Account in the bottom navigation.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 - By email (if you cannot access the app)

This path is for users who have lost access to their device or can't open the app. Because email is not how you sign in, an email claim alone isn't enough to prove account ownership - so before we process anything we verify by SMS to the phone number on file.

  1. Email aho.hunts@gmail.com from any email address. Include the phone number you signed up with, with country code (e.g. +20 1XX XXX XXXX). Use the subject "Account deletion request".
  2. We'll send a 6-digit verification code by SMS to that phone number, usually within 24 hours.
  3. Reply to our email with the verification code to confirm the request.
  4. Once verified, your account is deleted within 7 days and we reply to confirm.

If you don't receive an SMS within 24 hours, reply to the email and we'll follow up.

What gets deleted

  • Account and authentication credentials
  • Profile information (display name, photo, phone number)
  • Submission photos
  • Achievements and notification history

What is retained

  • Hunts you won remain publicly visible, with your name and photo removed (replaced with "Deleted user"), to preserve hunt history integrity.
  • Submission records may be retained in anonymized form for up to 90 days for fraud prevention, then permanently deleted.

Questions

Any questions about deletion or your data: aho.hunts@gmail.com.
